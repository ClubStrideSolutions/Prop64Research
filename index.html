Critical Data Collection Actions

CRITICAL Immediate Actions (0-3 Months)

Action 1: Implement Pre/Post Workshop Assessments

Gap: No baseline data on participant knowledge, attitudes, or behaviors. Cannot measure workshop impact without pre/post assessments.

Solution: Design and implement comprehensive assessment protocol:

  • Pre-Workshop (5 minutes): Knowledge of substance harms, perceived access, past 30-day use (confidential), intentions to use
  • Post-Workshop (Immediate): Knowledge gains, perception changes, confidence to resist peer pressure
  • Follow-Up (30-90 days): Self-reported behavior, help-seeking actions, academic engagement

Timeline: Design by November 2025, pilot by December 2025, full implementation by January 2026

  • Youth arrest trends by age and neighborhood
  • Cannabis-related incidents (2021-2025 update)
  • Gang-related incidents by location
